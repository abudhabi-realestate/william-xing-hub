# Golden Visa — Documents & Process

> Applies to **property investment** Golden Visa applications.  
> Document checklists and processing times vary slightly by emirate and application channel. Confirm with GDRFA / ICA and policy at the time of application. This does not constitute legal or investment advice.

---

## 1. Principal Applicant Documents (Real Estate Investor)

| No. | Document | Notes |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | **Passport copy** | Valid for the residency period required |
| 2 | **Title Deed** | Property value not less than **2 million AED** |
| 3 | **Passport-style photo** | White background, per visa centre specifications |
| 4 | **Proof of payment** | One of the following, depending on payment method:<br>· **Off-plan:** Account Statement (payment records / transaction history)<br>· **Mortgage:** Bank-issued **NOC** (No Objection Certificate) |
| 5 | **Local health insurance** | Health insurance policy recognised in the UAE |
| 6 | **Family members’ passport copies** | If dependants apply together, see Section 2 below |

---

## 2. Family Member Documents

In addition to dependants’ passport copies, provide the following by relationship (**all require notarisation and legalisation**):

| Relationship | Required Documents |
|------|----------|
| **Spouse** | Marriage certificate |
| **Children** | Birth certificate; **unmarried certificate** additionally required for children **aged 18 and above** |
| **Mother as principal applicant (Sponsor)** | **No-objection letter** from the father |
| **Parents** | Sponsor’s **birth certificate** (to establish parent–child relationship) |

> **Notarisation & legalisation:** Documents issued abroad typically require notarisation in the country of origin plus legalisation by the UAE embassy/consulate locally. Confirm exact requirements with the application service centre.

---

## 3. End-to-End Process

| Step | Description | Reference Timeline |
|------|------|----------|
| 1 | **Submit documents** | Via developer channel, registered broker, or designated GDRFA / ICA service centre |
| 2 | **Government approval** | Approximately **half a day to 1 day** |
| 3 | **Medical examination** | Approximately **half a day** (designated medical centre) |
| 4 | **Biometrics** | Approximately **half a day** |
| 5 | **Collect visa / physical Emirates ID card** | Typically several business days; **paid expedite available same day** |

## 5. Link to Application Thresholds

Before preparing documents, confirm that payment milestones meet your emirate’s requirements (see [Golden Visa — FAQ](./golden-visa-guide.html)):

- **Dubai:** Apply once the down payment is paid (mortgage requires bank NOC)
- **Abu Dhabi:** Apply once cumulative payments reach at least **2 million AED**
- **Multiple properties:** May be combined to meet the threshold
