# Abu Dhabi Golden Visa vs Dubai Golden Visa

> Policies may change. Confirm with GDRFA / ICA and the service centre at the time of application. This does not constitute legal or investment advice.

---

## Core Comparison

The UAE Golden Visa shares a federal framework — **10-year long-term residency, property threshold 2 million AED**.

However, **Abu Dhabi and Dubai** currently differ materially in application conditions and what happens after you sell the property.

Many ask only “can I get it with 2 million?” The better questions are: **how much must be paid before applying? Can I sell the property later and keep the visa?**

| | **Abu Dhabi** | **Dubai** |
|---|-------------|---------|
| **Property value** | ≥ 2 million AED | ≥ 2 million AED |
| **Payment requirement** | Must have **paid ≥ 2 million AED** | **Down payment paid** is sufficient to apply |
| **Visa after selling property** | **Remains valid** | **Invalidated** |

**In three sentences:**

- Abu Dhabi: **value met + payments met** — higher bar, but **you can sell the property and keep the visa**  
- Dubai: **value met + down payment in place** — lower bar, but **sell the property and the visa is gone**

---

## Abu Dhabi

Application conditions (property investment route):

1. Property value **exceeds 2 million AED**  
2. Cumulative payments of **at least 2 million AED** (with verifiable payment records)  
3. Property must be in a **freehold** area

**Key difference from other emirates:**

The property **may be resold**. After sale, the **Golden Visa remains valid** — your status is not locked to that single unit.

Best suited for: buyers who have paid the full 2 million AED, want future flexibility to dispose of the asset, and wish to retain long-term residency.

---

## Dubai

Application conditions (property investment route):

1. Property value **exceeds 2 million AED**  
2. **Down payment paid** is sufficient to apply (mortgage requires bank NOC)  
3. Property must be in a **freehold** area

**Key difference from other emirates:**

**The Golden Visa is invalidated upon resale** — status is tied to property ownership; you cannot “get the visa and sell immediately.”

Best suited for: buyers planning long-term holding, or those who accept “sell property = give up visa.”

## 2026 Policy Update · China Passport Holders

Latest immigration requirements:

For **China passport** holders applying for a Golden Visa **after 15 November 2026**, in addition to passport, photos, and other baseline documents, you must also provide:

**Police clearance certificate (certificate of no criminal record)**

Obtain and complete notarisation/legalisation in China in advance to avoid delays at submission. Format and validity period are subject to the service centre’s requirements at the time of application.

## What Is the Same

Both cities align on the following:

- 10-year validity, renewable subject to conditions  
- Not tied to an employer  
- Investment category generally has no mandatory physical presence requirement  
- Multiple properties may be combined to reach 2 million AED  
- Dependants (spouse, children, etc.) may apply together  

More FAQs: **[Golden Visa — FAQ](./golden-visa-guide.html)**.

---

## How to Choose

- Want **earlier status, lower upfront payment pressure** → consider Dubai  
- Want to **sell after paying 2 million AED while keeping residency** → consider Abu Dhabi  
- **China passport, applying after 15 November** → arrange **police clearance certificate** in advance  

The Golden Visa is a long-term residency tool, not a passport. Before choosing a city, ask: **how long do you plan to hold this property?**
